My Pet Nala

About Nala

Nala is a Jack Russel Terrier breed of dog. She is my favorite all time and my bestfriend

Nala likes wagging her tail anytime she's excited or see a friendly human. She is friendly herself. Sometimes Nala gets lonely and you can know this when you see her body shaking or shivering.

Her favorite things throughout the day include:

  • Chasing cars along the fence.
  • Barking to birds and strange animals
  • Being pet on the back and stomach
  • Meal time

Hmm, what does Nala play with?

  1. Balls and anything round
  2. Shoes, sandals and slippers
  3. Bone

Guys, you'll love Nala and she will love you too especially if you like games you and him will sync just fine.
